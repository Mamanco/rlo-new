At the recent Legal 500 GC Summit, our founder and managing partner, Omar Alrasheed, was joined by Majed Alharbi, CEO of Areeb Capital, for an in-depth examination of the Kingdom’s booming real estate market. The panel, moderated by Isabel Caine, explored the critical relationship between in-house counsel and senior management in large-scale developments. The discussion provided valuable case studies on resolving construction disputes, building stakeholder trust, and effectively translating complex legal requirements into strategic business outcomes.
